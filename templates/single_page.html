We've got, what you need!

Schoolchap is an application to manage school process online. Through this, schools can automate all their process from teachers/students management to informing parents about their child's performance or behaviour or wellness.

Our Awesome Features

Attendance System

Teachers can take daily attendance and parents will be informed about their child's presence/absence through push notifications.

Event Updates

School can inform about live/future events and updates to parents and teachers, so that they don't miss any important events.

Concise & Beautiful Dashboard

School admin can know the status of the school at any point of time through the graphical and statistical manner. Also each and every student's performance can be monitored.

Online Fee Payment

School can manage student's fee payment records while parents can pay their child's fee through web or app.
